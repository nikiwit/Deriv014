# Deriv Solutions Sdn Bhd - Leave Policies

## Annual Leave

- **Less than 2 years:** 8 days
- **2-5 years:** 12 days
- **More than 5 years:** 16 days
- **Application:** Submit via HR portal at least 7 days in advance
- **Unused leave:** Carry forward max 5 days to next year

## Sick Leave (with medical certificate)

- **Less than 2 years:** 14 days
- **2-5 years:** 18 days
- **More than 5 years:** 22 days
- **Hospitalization:** Up to 60 days
- **Notification:** Inform manager by 9 AM on first day of absence

## Medical Leave Without Certificate

- Maximum 2 days per occasion for non-hospitalization illness

## Maternity Leave

- 98 consecutive days (paid) for eligible female employees
- **Eligibility:** Employed at least 90 days before confinement
- **Application:** Submit Form 14A at least 30 days before expected delivery

## Paternity Leave

- 7 consecutive days (paid) for married male employees
- Must be taken within 60 days of childbirth
- **Application:** At least 7 days notice with marriage and birth certificates

## Public Holidays

- 11 paid public holidays (as gazetted for Selangor)
- If holiday falls on rest day, following working day is off

## Compassionate Leave

- 3 days (paid) for immediate family member's death (parent, spouse, child, sibling)
- Submit death certificate within 7 days

## Marriage Leave

- 3 days (paid) for employee's own marriage
- Once per employment with the company
- Submit marriage certificate copy

## Unpaid Leave

- Available upon manager approval for exceptional circumstances
- Does not accrue toward annual leave calculations
