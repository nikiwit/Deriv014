# Deriv Solutions Sdn Bhd - Employee Handbook

## 1. Welcome Message

Welcome to Deriv! We're excited to have you join our team. This handbook outlines our policies and your rights as an employee under Malaysian employment law.

## 2. Employment Relationship

- **At-Will Employment:** Not applicable in Malaysia. Employment is governed by the Employment Act 1955 and your employment contract.
- **Probation Period:** 3 months for all new hires. Performance review at end of probation.
- **Classification:** Full-time permanent employment.

## 3. Working Hours & Attendance

- **Standard Hours:** 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday (1-hour lunch break)
- **Core Hours:** 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM (must be present/online during hybrid work)
- **Workweek:** 40 hours/week, 8 hours/day
- **Overtime:** Paid at 1.5x hourly rate (first 4 hours), 2x thereafter. Requires manager approval.
- **Attendance:** Clock in/out via HR portal. Late arrival (>15 mins) requires notification to manager.

## 4. Code of Conduct & Ethics

- **Professional Behavior:** Treat all colleagues with respect. Discrimination or harassment of any kind is prohibited.
- **Anti-Harassment:** Zero tolerance for sexual harassment, bullying, or hostile work environment.
- **Conflict of Interest:** Disclose any outside work or business interests that may conflict with company duties.
- **Confidentiality:** Protect company and client data. Non-disclosure agreement is part of your contract.
- **Social Media:** Do not disclose confidential company information. Personal views do not represent the company.

## 5. Compensation & Benefits

- **Payroll:** Processed monthly by the 25th of each month via bank transfer.
- **EPF/SOCSO:** Statutory contributions made as per Malaysian law.
- **Annual Increment:** Performance-based review in January each year.

## 6. Performance Management

- **Probation Review:** Month 3
- **Annual Review:** January (linked to increment and bonus decisions)
- **Goal Setting:** Quarterly OKRs set with your manager
- **Development Plans:** Discussed during annual review

## 7. Disciplinary Procedure

- **Minor Issues:** Verbal warning → Written warning → Final warning
- **Serious Misconduct:** May result in immediate termination (theft, fraud, violence, gross insubordination)
- **Appeal Process:** Submit written appeal to HR within 7 days

## 8. Grievance Handling

- Submit concerns to your manager or HR in writing
- HR will investigate within 14 days
- Confidentiality maintained throughout process

## 9. Termination & Resignation

- **Notice Period:** 1 month for both employer and employee
- **Exit Interview:** Conducted by HR
- **Final Settlement:** Paid within 7 days of last working day (unused leave, pro-rated bonus if applicable)
