# Deriv Solutions Sdn Bhd - Data Privacy & IT Security Policy

## Data Privacy Policy (PDPA Compliance)

### 1. Personal Data Collection

**We collect:** Name, NRIC, address, phone, email, bank details, emergency contacts, educational certificates, employment history.

**Purpose:** Payroll processing, EPF/SOCSO/LHDN submissions, benefits administration, emergency contact, performance management.

**Consent:** By signing your employment contract, you consent to this collection and processing.

### 2. Data Disclosure

Your data will NOT be shared with third parties except:
- Government bodies (EPF, SOCSO, LHDN, Immigration)
- Insurance providers (for medical coverage)
- As required by Malaysian law

### 3. Your Rights

- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data via HR
- **Correction:** Request corrections to inaccurate data
- **Withdrawal:** You may withdraw consent (may affect employment status)

### 4. Data Retention

- **Active employees:** Data retained throughout employment
- **Former employees:** 7 years post-termination (for tax and legal compliance)
- **Secure disposal:** Shredding (physical), secure deletion (digital)

### 5. Data Breach Protocol

- Immediate investigation by IT and HR
- Affected individuals notified within 72 hours
- Records retained for minimum 2 years
- Report to Personal Data Protection Commissioner if required

## IT Security Policy

### 1. Acceptable Use

- Company devices, email, and systems are for work purposes
- Personal use allowed within reason (no illegal activity, harassment, or excessive bandwidth use)
- **Prohibited:** Downloading pirated software, accessing inappropriate content, sharing credentials

### 2. Password Requirements

- Minimum 10 characters (uppercase, lowercase, number, special character)
- Change every 90 days
- Do not share passwords or write them down
- Use password manager (company-provided: 1Password)

### 3. Data Handling

- **Approved Storage:** Google Workspace, AWS S3 (company accounts only)
- **Prohibited:** Storing company data on personal Dropbox, USB drives, or personal email
- **Sensitive Data:** Client information, source code, financial records must be encrypted

### 4. Remote Work Security

- Use company VPN when accessing internal systems
- Secure home Wi-Fi with WPA3 encryption
- Lock screen when away from device (max 5 min timeout)
- Do not work from public Wi-Fi without VPN

### 5. Device Security

- **Company laptops:** Full disk encryption enabled
- **Personal devices:** If used for work email, must have PIN/biometric lock
- **Lost/stolen device:** Report to IT immediately (24/7 hotline: +60 12-345 6789)

### 6. Incident Reporting

**Report immediately to security@Deriv.com.my:**
- Lost or stolen devices
- Suspicious emails (phishing attempts)
- Unauthorized access to your accounts
- Any security concerns

**Violations:** May result in disciplinary action up to termination.s
