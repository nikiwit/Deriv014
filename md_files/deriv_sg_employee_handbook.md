# Deriv Solutions Pte Ltd - Employee Handbook

## 1. Welcome

Welcome to Deriv Solutions Singapore! We're excited to have you join our growing regional team. This handbook outlines company policies and your employment terms under the Employment Act (Cap. 91) of Singapore.

As part of the Deriv Group, you'll work alongside talented professionals across Malaysia and Singapore, delivering world-class cloud solutions to clients throughout ASEAN.

## 2. Employment Relationship

- **Contract Type:** Permanent employment with mutual termination rights as per contract
- **Probation:** 3 months for all new hires. Confirmation subject to satisfactory performance review.
- **Classification:** Full-time PMET (Professional, Manager, Executive, Technician)
- **Group Structure:** Deriv Solutions Pte Ltd is a Singapore entity under Deriv Group

## 3. Working Hours & Attendance

- **Standard Hours:** 44 hours/week, flexible schedule within 8:00 AM - 7:00 PM
- **Core Hours:** 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM (must be available during hybrid work)
- **Regional Collaboration:** Occasional meetings with Malaysia team (GMT+8 timezone alignment)
- **Overtime:** Not typically required for PMET roles. Exceptional hours may be compensated with time-off-in-lieu (subject to manager approval).
- **Attendance:** Log hours via HR platform. Notify manager of any schedule deviations.

## 4. Code of Conduct

- **Workplace Respect:** Harassment, discrimination, and bullying are strictly prohibited. We maintain a diverse and inclusive environment across all Deriv offices.
- **Confidentiality:** Protect company IP, trade secrets, and client information per your NDA. This applies to both Singapore and Malaysia operations.
- **Conflict of Interest:** Declare any outside employment or business interests to HR.
- **Anti-Corruption:** No bribery, kickbacks, or improper payments. Comply with Singapore's Prevention of Corruption Act.
- **Social Media:** Exercise good judgment. Do not disclose confidential information or claim to represent the company without authorization.
- **Cross-Border Ethics:** Maintain consistent professional standards when working with regional teams and clients.

## 5. Compensation

- **Salary:** Paid monthly by 25th via GIRO
- **CPF:** Employer and employee contributions as per CPF Act
- **Annual Wage Supplement (AWS):** Paid in December (pro-rated for first year)
- **Performance Bonus:** Discretionary, based on company and individual performance (typically 1-2 months for individual contributors, up to 3 months for senior roles)
- **Regional Alignment:** Compensation reviewed annually with consideration for regional market rates

## 6. Performance Management

- **Probation Review:** End of month 3
- **Annual Review:** Q1 each year (aligned with Malaysia HQ review cycle)
- **Quarterly Check-ins:** Goal setting and progress reviews with direct manager
- **360 Feedback:** Collected in Q4 to inform annual review
- **Development:** Career conversations quarterly; access to Deriv Group training programs

## 7. Disciplinary Actions

- **Process:** Counseling → Written warning → Final warning → Termination
- **Serious Misconduct:** May result in summary dismissal (fraud, theft, violence, gross negligence, serious breach of confidentiality, violation of client contracts)
- **Fair Process:** Employee will be informed of allegations and given opportunity to respond
- **Appeals:** May escalate to Singapore management or Group HR

## 8. Grievances

- Raise concerns with direct manager or Singapore HR
- Formal grievances: Submit in writing to hr.sg@Deriv.com.sg
- Investigation completed within 21 days
- Escalation path: Singapore Manager → Singapore Director → Group HR (Malaysia)
- Confidentiality maintained throughout process

## 9. Termination & Resignation

- **Notice Period:** 1 month (both parties), or as specified in employment contract
- **Garden Leave:** Company may place employee on garden leave during notice period
- **Exit Process:** Return all company property (Singapore and any Malaysia-issued equipment), attend exit interview, complete knowledge transfer
- **Final Pay:** Within 7 days of last working day, includes pro-rated annual leave, AWS (if applicable), and performance bonus (if applicable)
- **Post-Employment Obligations:** Non-compete and confidentiality clauses remain in effect as per contract

## 10. Regional Collaboration

- **Cross-Border Projects:** You may work on projects with Malaysia-based colleagues
- **Travel:** Occasional business travel to Malaysia HQ may be required (travel expenses covered)
- **Communication:** Use approved platforms (Slack, Google Meet) for regional collaboration
- **Time Zones:** Both offices operate in GMT+8 for seamless coordination
