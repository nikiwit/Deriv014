# Deriv Solutions Pte Ltd - Data Privacy & IT Security Policy

## Personal Data Protection Policy (PDPA Compliance)

### 1. Collection Notice

**We collect:** Name, NRIC/FIN, contact details, bank account, educational qualifications, employment history, emergency contacts, medical information (for insurance claims).

**Purpose:** Employment administration, payroll, CPF submissions, benefits, performance management, compliance with Employment Act and other regulations.

**Consent:** Signing your employment contract constitutes consent. You may withdraw consent (may impact employment).

### 2. Use & Disclosure

Personal data used only for stated purposes. Disclosed to:
- CPF Board, IRAS (tax authority), MOM (Ministry of Manpower)
- Insurance providers (for group insurance)
- Third-party payroll processors (under strict confidentiality agreements)

We do NOT sell your data to third parties.

### 3. Data Subject Rights

- **Access:** Request copy of your data (email hr.sg@deriv.com.sg)
- **Correction:** Request updates to inaccurate information
- **Portability:** Request data in machine-readable format (where technically feasible)
- **Withdrawal:** Withdraw consent (subject to legal and contractual restrictions)

**Response time:** 30 days

### 4. Data Protection

- Stored on secure servers with encryption
- Access limited to HR and authorized personnel only
- Regular security audits conducted

### 5. Retention

- **Current employees:** Throughout employment
- **Former employees:** 5 years post-termination (statutory requirement for CPF, tax records)
- Secure destruction after retention period

### 6. Data Breach Response

- Immediate containment and investigation
- Notification to affected individuals within 72 hours
- Notification to Personal Data Protection Commission (PDPC) if significant harm likely
- Breach records maintained for 3 years

### 7. Contact

**Data Protection Officer:** dpo.sg@deriv.com.sg

## IT Security Policy

### 1. Acceptable Use

- Company IT resources for business purposes
- Limited personal use acceptable (no illegal, offensive, or bandwidth-intensive activities)
- **Prohibited:** Unauthorized software installation, cryptocurrency mining, sharing credentials

### 2. Access Control

- **Passwords:** Minimum 12 characters, mix of character types
- **Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA):** Required for all work accounts
- **Password Changes:** Every 90 days or immediately if compromised
- **Biometric/Hardware Keys:** Encouraged for critical systems

### 3. Data Classification & Handling

- **Public:** Marketing materials, public website content
- **Internal:** General business communications
- **Confidential:** Client data, source code, financial records (encryption required)
- **Strictly Confidential:** Trade secrets, unreleased products (additional access controls)

**Approved Storage:** Google Workspace, AWS (company accounts), approved project management tools

**Prohibited:** Personal cloud storage, unauthorized file-sharing services, personal email for work files

### 4. Remote Work Security

- **VPN:** Mandatory for accessing internal systems remotely
- **Home Network:** WPA3 encryption recommended; avoid public Wi-Fi without VPN
- **Screen Privacy:** Use privacy screens in shared spaces, lock screen when unattended
- **Physical Security:** Keep devices secure; do not leave unattended in public

### 5. Device Management

- **Company Laptops:** MDM (Mobile Device Management) enrolled, full disk encryption, auto-lock after 5 minutes
- **Personal Devices:** If used for work email (BYOD), must meet minimum security standards: PIN/biometric lock, OS updates current, remote wipe capability enabled
- **Lost/Stolen:** Report immediately to IT (24/7: +65 9123 4567) for remote lock/wipe

### 6. Email & Communication Security

- Be vigilant for phishing: Check sender addresses, don't click suspicious links
- Report phishing attempts to security@deriv.com.sg
- Encrypt sensitive information sent externally (use company-approved encryption tools)

### 7. Software & Updates

- Use only company-approved software (see IT portal for list)
- Enable automatic OS and application updates
- No personal software on company devices without IT approval

### 8. Incident Reporting

**Report immediately** to security@deriv.com.sg or IT Helpdesk:
- Lost/stolen devices
- Suspected malware or security breach
- Phishing attempts or suspicious emails
- Unauthorized access to systems or data
- Any security policy violations

**Response:** IT will investigate, contain threat, and coordinate with management and DPO as needed.

**Non-Compliance:** Security violations may result in disciplinary action, including termination, and may be reported to authorities if criminal activity is suspected.
