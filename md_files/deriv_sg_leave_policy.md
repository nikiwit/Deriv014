# Deriv Solutions Pte Ltd - Leave Policies

## Annual Leave

- **Year 1:** 7 days
- **Year 2:** 8 days
- **Year 3:** 9 days
- **Year 4:** 10 days
- **Year 5:** 11 days
- **Year 6:** 12 days
- **Year 7:** 13 days
- **Year 8 and above:** 14 days
- **Application:** Submit via HR portal at least 7 days in advance
- **Unused leave:** Carry forward max 5 days to next year (encashment not applicable)

## Sick Leave (with medical certificate)

- **Outpatient:** 14 days per year
- **Hospitalization:** Up to 60 days per year (inclusive of outpatient days)
- **Notification:** Inform manager by 9 AM on first day of absence
- **Medical Certificate:** Must be from a company-approved or government clinic/hospital

## Maternity Leave

- 16 weeks (Government-Paid Maternity Leave for first and second child)
- **First 8 weeks:** Paid by employer
- **Last 8 weeks:** Reimbursed by government (capped at $10,000 per 4 weeks)
- **Third child onwards:** 16 weeks fully government-paid
- **Eligibility:** Employed at least 3 months before delivery, child is a Singapore citizen
- **Application:** Notify HR at least 4 weeks before expected delivery

## Paternity Leave

- 2 weeks (Government-Paid Paternity Leave)
- **Eligibility:** Married father, child is a Singapore citizen, employed at least 3 months
- Must be taken within 16 weeks of childbirth
- **Application:** At least 1 week notice with marriage and birth certificates

## Shared Parental Leave

- Working fathers may share up to 4 weeks of the mother's 16-week maternity leave
- Subject to mutual agreement and employer approval

## Childcare Leave

- 6 days per year for employees with children under 7 years old
- **Eligibility:** Employed at least 3 months, child is a Singapore citizen
- **First 3 days:** Employer-paid
- **Last 3 days:** Government-paid (capped at $500 per day)

## Extended Childcare Leave

- 2 days per year for employees with children aged 7-12
- **Employer-paid:** First day
- **Government-paid:** Second day (capped at $500)

## Public Holidays

- 11 gazetted public holidays per year
- If holiday falls on rest day or non-working day, the next working day is a paid holiday

## Compassionate Leave

- 3 days (paid) for death of immediate family member (parent, spouse, child, sibling)
- Submit supporting documents within 7 days

## Marriage Leave

- 3 days (paid) for employee's own marriage
- Once per employment with the company
- Submit marriage certificate copy

## National Service Leave

- Reservist/NS obligations: Leave granted as required by MINDEF
- Salary top-up: Company will top up difference between NS pay and regular salary

## Unpaid Leave

- Available upon manager and HR approval for exceptional circumstances
- Does not accrue toward annual leave calculations
- Must exhaust all annual leave before applying
